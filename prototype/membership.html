Questions

Frequently asked

Does a paid plan affect my school's rating?

No. Ever. Memberships affect placement, listing richness, and access to tools — but ratings are generated purely from verified family reviews. See our editorial policy.

How do you verify reviews?

Every reviewer must confirm their relationship to the school — usually via a tuition receipt, a school-issued email, or an in-person verification through our local ambassadors. About 9% of submitted reviews fail verification and are never published.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel from your admin dashboard any time. Your paid features stay active until the end of your current billing period. No refunds for partial periods, but no hidden fees either.

What counts as a "school"?

Full-time K–12 Islamic schools, preschools, seminaries, and full-time hifz programs. For weekend-only schools and tutoring centers, contact us — we have a separate (cheaper) plan.

Do I need to pay to respond to reviews?

Yes, from Basic tier ($299/year) upward. Free listings can receive reviews but can't respond publicly. We do this so schools that engage have a real track record with us, which reduces review-response abuse.

Is there a contract or setup fee?

No setup fee and no long-term contract. You pay annually (or monthly if you prefer), and you can cancel any time.

Can multiple schools in a network share one plan?

Each school needs its own listing and plan. If you operate a network of 5+ schools, email us at schools@islamicschoolreview.com for multi-school pricing.

What's the refund policy?

We offer a 14-day refund for new subscriptions, no questions asked. After 14 days, or at renewal, fees are non-refundable — but you keep your benefits until the period ends.

Not sure which plan is right?

Book a 15-minute call with our school-success team. We'll walk through your situation and recommend a plan — or tell you to stick with free.
