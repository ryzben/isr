My son has been at Al-Furqan for two years now and the transformation has been beautiful, mashallah. The Quran program is the main reason we chose it — teachers are patient but expect real effort. Academics are solid; math and science are strong. Tuition is reasonable compared to what we looked at. We'd like to see more extracurriculars and a dedicated gym space.
