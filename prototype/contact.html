Common questions

Before you reach out

How do I claim my school's listing?

Find your school in the directory, open its profile, and click "Claim this listing." We verify via an official school email and respond within one business day.

Can I remove a negative review?

Not for being negative. We only remove reviews that violate our guidelines (personal attacks, unverifiable claims, fake reviews). Real, verified criticism stays — that's the point.

Do premium listings affect ratings?

No. Premium listings affect placement and profile richness, never ratings. See our editorial policy.

Do you accept guest posts?

Yes — from educators, administrators, and parents with something concrete to say. Pitch us at editorial@islamicschoolreview.com with a one-paragraph summary.

Can you help me choose a school?

We can't make personal recommendations, but our guides walk through the decision in detail.

I found a factual error. What do I do?

Email editorial@islamicschoolreview.com with the URL and the correction. We publish corrections promptly.
