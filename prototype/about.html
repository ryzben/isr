Our story

Built by parents who couldn't find what they needed.

Islamic School Review started with a simple frustration: when a family moves to a new city, there's no easy way to find and compare Islamic schools. Websites are outdated. Reviews are scattered across Google, Facebook groups, and word-of-mouth. Schools can't easily reach the right families, and great teachers can't easily find the right roles.

We set out to fix that. One verified review, one updated school profile, one honest article at a time.

"Find the school. Find the teacher. Find the community."

That's the short version of why we exist.

"We wanted the site we wished existed when we were searching for our kids' school."

How we work

Editorial & review policy — in plain English

Read the full policy →

1 How reviews are verified

  • Every reviewer confirms their email before a review posts.
  • Parents can optionally upload proof of enrollment for a "Verified parent" badge.
  • Suspected fake or duplicate reviews are removed after human review.
  • Schools can flag a review; we investigate, but we never delete criticism just because it's unflattering.

2 What premium membership does not buy

  • A higher star rating — ratings come only from parent reviews.
  • The ability to remove negative reviews.
  • Placement in editorial articles (those are marked as "Sponsored" when sponsored).
  • Any editorial influence — reviews, rankings, and articles are independent of sales.

3 How sponsored content is labeled

  • All sponsored articles carry a clear "Sponsored" tag in the byline, category, and URL.
  • Sponsors don't get approval over editorial content — only over their own sponsored pieces.
  • Featured school carousels are labeled as promotional placements, not rankings.

4 How we handle corrections

  • Schools can submit correction requests for factual errors on their profile.
  • Any substantive correction to an article is noted at the bottom of that article, with date.
  • Appeals for takedowns go to a three-person editorial board, not to sales.
