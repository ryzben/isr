What makes a helpful review

  • Specific examples from your own experience
  • Both strengths and areas for growth
  • Balanced tone — warm but honest
  • Context about grade level and years attended
  • Focus on programs, culture, and systems

What we remove

  • Names of individual students or staff
  • Personal attacks, harassment, or threats
  • Content that could identify minors
  • Hearsay or second-hand accounts
  • Promotional or paid content
